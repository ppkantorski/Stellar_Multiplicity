- get familiar with stellar multiplicity and environment dependence

- look at reduced images; do a visual inspection to check multiplicity status and approximate precision of relative astrometry/photometry

- literature search on clusters: up-to-date info on distance, age,
extinction, [NIR] color-magnitude diagrams, ...

- determine star properties (Teff, photometry): SIMBAD, plus cluster papers

- generate appropriate isochrones and derive stellar masses for primaries

- determine masses for secondaries

- compute detection limits in flux ratio and in mass ratio

- Malmquist bias evaluation

- chance alignment analysis, proper motion confirmation for wide companions?

- statistical analysis: BF, sep distribution, q distribution  (Bayesian?)
